<threat_model>
Trust boundaries:
- Drag-and-drop crosses from the app into Finder, Cubase, or another app.
- Output inbox items may become stale if files are deleted or moved after conversion.
- Converter rows may contain source URLs and temp paths that must not be exposed as ready WAV outputs.

STRIDE register:
- threat_id: T-03-HANDOFF
  category: Information Disclosure
  component: Drag providers
  disposition: mitigate
  severity: medium
  mitigation_plan: Drag only verified output URLs whose files exist; never drag source URLs, temp URLs, failed rows, or metadata strings.
- threat_id: T-03-MISSING
  category: Tampering
  component: OutputInboxInspectorView
  disposition: mitigate
  severity: medium
  mitigation_plan: Refresh availability and hide drag/reveal readiness for `.missing`, `.pending`, or `.failed` items.
- threat_id: T-03-REVEAL
  category: Repudiation
  component: Reveal in Finder
  disposition: mitigate
  severity: low
  mitigation_plan: Reveal uses the exact verified output URL visible in the row and does not mutate files.
</threat_model>
