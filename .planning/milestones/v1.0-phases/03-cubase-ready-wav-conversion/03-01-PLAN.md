<threat_model>
Trust boundaries:
- Source audio files are untrusted local user input and may be malformed, unsupported, huge, or mislabeled.
- The output folder is user-controlled local storage.
- The native converter crosses into AVFAudio and filesystem side effects.

STRIDE register:
- threat_id: T-03-WAVSPEC
  category: Tampering
  component: WAVOutputVerifier
  disposition: mitigate
  severity: high
  mitigation_plan: Verify file existence, WAV/PCM format, sample rate, bit depth, and channel count before returning success or allowing output inbox readiness.
- threat_id: T-03-SOURCE-SAFETY
  category: Tampering
  component: NativeAudioConverter
  disposition: mitigate
  severity: medium
  mitigation_plan: Read sources only, write to a temporary output URL, and move only verified WAVs to the final destination.
- threat_id: T-03-MEMORY
  category: Denial of Service
  component: NativeAudioConverter
  disposition: mitigate
  severity: medium
  mitigation_plan: Stream bounded `AVAudioPCMBuffer` chunks instead of reading full audio files into memory.
</threat_model>
